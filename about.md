I'm Anenth — Co-Founder at [Premagic](https://www.premagic.com), an AI-powered event technology platform that turns attendees into event marketers. With over a decade of experience building products for the web, I now channel that expertise into building and scaling Premagic.

My journey began with CSS and the sheer magic of making things come alive in the browser. That early fascination evolved into a deep passion for front-end engineering and product development — from crafting pixel-perfect interfaces to architecting scalable, performant web applications and ultimately co-founding a product company.

## What I do

At Premagic, I lead product development and front-end architecture, building an AI-powered platform that helps event organizers boost engagement through smart photo distribution, personalized branding, and attendee advocacy tools.

My focus areas include:

- **Product Engineering** — Turning complex problems into delightful user experiences
- **Front-End Architecture** — Building scalable, performant web applications with modern technologies
- **AI-Powered Experiences** — Leveraging AI for photo recognition, personalization, and automation
- **Design Systems** — Building and scaling component libraries that teams love to use

## Philosophy

I believe great products are built at the intersection of engineering excellence, user empathy, and business impact. Code should be readable, maintainable, and a joy to work with. As a founder, I care deeply about building technology that creates real value — both for the teams I work with and for the customers we serve.
